This site contains the syllabus, schedule, and assignments for PUBPL 6002: Data Science and Statistics for Public Management, held during Fall 2021 at the University of Utah.

By the end of this course, you will (1) be data literate and (2) be able to answer your own questions with statistical and data scientific tools.
